The Story

What Happened This Week

Week 3 was the week everything started to look real. Up until this point I had been writing HTML — just structure, no soul. Then CSS happened. I wrote my first rule, refreshed the browser, and watched the page change. That feeling was something else.

I spent most of the lab figuring out the box model and why my elements kept overflowing. Once I understood padding versus margin, things started falling into place. Flexbox felt like a superpower — suddenly I could align things without fighting the browser.

The hardest part was the form. Styling inputs and getting the focus states right took longer than I expected. But finishing it and seeing a clean, styled contact form on my page made the whole lab worth it.
