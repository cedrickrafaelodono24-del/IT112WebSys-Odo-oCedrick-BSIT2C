The Story

What Happened This Week

Week 4 was the week everything stopped being static. I had HTML and CSS working the page looked good, but it couldn't do anything. Then JavaScript came in and changed that completely.

The first thing I built was the task manager. Creating elements with createElement, appending them to the list, and removing them on click, it felt like the page was actually responding to me. That was a completely different feeling from writing CSS.

The modal dialog was the part I was most proud of. Hiding and showing it by toggling a class, animating it in with CSS transitions, closing it on backdrop click or Escape key; it felt like I was building something real, not just a lab exercise.
